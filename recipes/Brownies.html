Brownies

This is a sample recipe for Brownies.

The BEST brownie recipe! With crispy edges, fudgy middles, and rich chocolate flavor, these homemade brownies will disappear in no time.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Powder
  • Eggs
  • FLour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. MIx chocolate powder, eggs and flour.
  2. Add to baking pan
  3. Heat in Oven for 15 min.
